How the trading post works

Whether you're stocking your kitchen or clearing your cellar, the process stays honest and direct.

01

Browse by region

Filter the shelf by category, village, or harvest year to find exactly the jar you grew up on.

02

Check the stamp

Every listing carries a provenance stamp — origin and harvest date, straight from the maker.

03

Trade directly

Your payment goes to the producer, minus a small trading fee. No warehouse markup in between.

01

List your batch

Tell us what you've made, how much, and when it was put up. Photos optional — honesty isn't.

02

Set your terms

You set the price, the jar size, and how many you're willing to part with this season.

03

Ship or hand-deliver

Fulfill orders on your own schedule — weekly, seasonally, or whenever the batch is ready.
